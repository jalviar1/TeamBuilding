Classification Rules

Helpful Notes:
Classifications will be OPEN for Freshmen, Sophomore, Junior and Senior Divisions ONLY. The lowest classification will be Freshman. Paddlers will declare their classification at the beginning of each racing season.
All paddlers may race two times, but only three paddlers per crew may move, not all six.
Age groups paddlers 12 through 16 shall be allowed to paddle in two classified events and be able to return to their original classification at next regatta. Only three paddlers per crew are allowed to move to the same second event.
Age group paddlers who have not reached their 18th or 19th birthday on the first day of the calendar year shall be allowed to paddle in two classified events and be able to return to their original classification at next regatta. Only three paddlers per crew are allowed to move to the same second event.
Novice A paddlers shall be allowed to paddle in one classified event and one unclassified event. Only three paddlers per crew are allowed to move to the same second event.
Novice B paddlers shall be allowed to paddle in one classified event and one unclassified event. Only three paddlers per crew are allowed to move to the same second event.
